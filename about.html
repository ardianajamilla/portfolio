My Story

My name is Ibra, though I often go by the pseudonym Ibraheem online. My journey with computers began at a very young age, guided by my father who is highly skilled in the world of technology. From those early days, he patiently taught me everything he knew, establishing a foundation that would shape my future.

I developed an intense passion for computing that has only grown stronger over time. I can easily spend hours or even an entire day immersed in front of my computer, completely absorbed in projects and learning. The digital world fascinates me in a way that few other things can.

That said, I'm not just a digital hermit. I enjoy getting out and spending time with friends, balancing my technological pursuits with real-world connections. But it's when I return to my computer that I truly feel in my element.

Over the years, I've devoted myself to mastering multiple programming languages. From web technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, to more complex languages like C#, PHP, and LUA. While I wouldn't claim complete fluency in all of them, I've developed strong proficiency that allows me to work effectively across different platforms and projects.

One of my favorite activities is creating LUA cheat scripts for GTA San Andreas. I've developed quite a collection - some I sell, others I share freely with the community. This venture has allowed me to combine my programming skills with my gaming interests in a way that's both creative and practical.

As I continue to grow and develop my skills, I remain passionate about coding, creating, and finding innovative solutions to complex problems. My journey from the lessons my father taught me in 2010 to where I am now has been filled with continuous learning and exploration, and I'm excited to see where this path leads next.
