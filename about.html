My Story

My name is Ibraheem (though my real name is Ibra). I have a deep passion for computers and technology that began in my early years.

Since 2010, I've been immersed in the digital world, learning everything I know from my father who is highly skilled with computers. Through his guidance, I've developed proficiency across various technologies.

I spend countless hours in front of the computer, creating and exploring. While I occasionally take breaks to socialize with friends, my true passion lies in coding and development.

I've mastered numerous programming languages including C#, JavaScript, CSS, HTML, and LUA. My specialty is creating LUA cheat scripts for GTA San Andreas, which I both sell and share freely with the community.

Though I may not be completely fluent in all languages yet, I'm constantly improving and expanding my skills to create better, more innovative solutions.
